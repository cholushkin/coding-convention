# Changelog

## [2.0.0]
- **Architecture Decoupling**: Separated project organization and structural rules from the core coding convention into independent documents.
- **Modern Standards**: Upgraded style guidelines to reflect modern C# syntax (such as file-scoped namespaces and auto-property serialization attributes) and optimized Unity performance workflows.
- **AI-Evaluated Choices**: All specific conventions were evaluated with the assistance of AI trained on a broad corpus of real-world software, ensuring our standards reflect well-established, industry-wide practices rather than isolated personal preferences.

## [1.0.0]
- **Legacy Framework**: Initial baseline convention reflecting standard legacy Unity/C# development patterns (circa 2018).
